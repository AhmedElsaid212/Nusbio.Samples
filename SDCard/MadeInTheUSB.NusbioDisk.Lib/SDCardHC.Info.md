# Nusbio Disk Design Info

## SD Card
Nusbio Disk is based on a Micro SD Card HC
Class 6 
- 4Gb up to 32 Gb

# Basic SD Card Knowledge

- SD CARD - 2 GB
- SD CARD HC (High Capacity) 4 to 32 GB
- SD CARD XC (Extended Capacity) 2GB to 2 TB (Theory)

- Class 2		- 2 Mb/s
- Class 4		- 4 Mb/s
- Class 6		- 6 Mb/s
- Class 10		- 10 Mb/s
- Class UHS-1	- 15 Mb/s
